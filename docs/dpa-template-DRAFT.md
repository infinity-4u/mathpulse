## Background

The School uses the Australian Maths App (the "Platform") to provide curriculum-aligned maths practice to its students. In doing so, the School acts as the data controller and the Processor acts as a data processor on behalf of the School.

This Agreement governs the processing of student personal information under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the [State] Privacy and Personal Information Protection Act [year] (where applicable), and any applicable state education department data governance policies.

---

## 1. Scope of processing

**Categories of data subjects:** Students enrolled at the School in Years 7–10, and their teachers and parents/guardians.

**Categories of personal information processed:**
- Teachers: name, email address
- Parents/guardians: name, email address
- Students: display name (nickname), year level (7–10), practice history (topics practised, accuracy, hints used)

**Expressly excluded:** The Processor does not collect and the School must not provide date of birth, home address, phone number, school-issued student IDs, government identifiers, or any sensitive information as defined by the Privacy Act.

**Purpose:** Providing the Platform's maths practice and progress-tracking functionality to the School's students and teachers.

---

## 2. Processor obligations

The Processor will:
- Process personal information only for the purposes described in this Agreement and the Platform's Privacy Policy
- Not sell, share, or disclose personal information to any third party not described in the Privacy Policy without the School's prior written consent
- Store all personal information in Australia (Supabase, Sydney region ap-southeast-2)
- Implement and maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures, including encryption at rest and in transit, and database-level access controls
- Delete all personal information relating to a student permanently and irreversibly within 30 days of receiving a written deletion request from the School or a parent/guardian
- Notify the School within 72 hours of becoming aware of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm
- Provide the School with reasonable assistance to respond to access and correction requests

---

## 3. School obligations

The School will:
- Ensure it has a valid basis for sharing student information with the Processor (e.g. inclusion in the School's privacy collection notice)
- Inform parents/guardians that the Platform is used and what information is collected, by reference to the Platform's Privacy Policy
- Only provide student information that is necessary for the Platform's operation — in practice, this means only creating student profiles with a nickname and year level
- Promptly notify the Processor of any deletion requests from parents/guardians

---

## 4. Data residency

All personal information is stored in Australia. The Processor will not transfer personal information outside Australia without prior written consent from the School, except to the sub-processors listed below who meet Australian Privacy Principle 8 requirements.

**Sub-processors:**
| Provider | Purpose | Location |
|---|---|---|
| Supabase | Database and authentication | Sydney, Australia (ap-southeast-2) |
| [Email provider] | Transactional email | [AU/EU] |

The Processor will notify the School before adding any new sub-processor that will process student personal information.

---

## 5. Audit and compliance

The Processor will, upon reasonable written notice, provide the School with information reasonably necessary to demonstrate compliance with this Agreement and applicable privacy laws. The School may conduct or commission an audit no more than once per calendar year.

---

## 6. Term and termination

This Agreement commences on the date of the School's first use of the Platform and continues until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, the Processor will permanently delete all School personal information within 30 days and provide written confirmation of deletion.
