## What information we collect

We collect the minimum necessary to operate the Platform.

**From teachers and parents (account holders):**
- Full name
- Email address

**From students (via teacher or parent registration):**
- Display name (a nickname — a real name is not required)
- Year level (7, 8, 9, or 10)

**We never collect:** date of birth, home address, phone number, school-issued IDs, Medicare numbers, or any other government identifier.

**Usage data:** We record which topics a student has practised, their accuracy, and the number of hints used per question. We do not record keystrokes, timing at the individual-question level, or any data beyond what is needed to show progress.

---

## How we use your information

- To create and manage teacher, parent, and student accounts
- To display student progress to the student, their teacher, and their linked parent
- To allow teachers to assign topics and view class progress
- To allow parents to view their child's practice history

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing profiling, or any purpose not described above. We do not sell or share your data with any third party not listed in this policy.

## How long we keep your data

We retain account data for as long as the account is active. When an account is deleted, all associated data is permanently and irreversibly removed ("hard deleted") — including student profiles, practice history, and question attempts. We do not retain backups of deleted records beyond our standard backup rotation period of [X days].

## Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), you may:
- Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact [email]. We will respond within 30 days. Deletion requests are completed within 30 days and confirmed in writing.

## Student accounts and parental consent

Students do not create accounts directly. All student accounts are created by a teacher (in a school context) or a parent/guardian. By creating a student profile, the registering adult confirms they have authority to do so.

For students under 15, the adult creating the account acts as the consenting party for the collection and use of the student's data as described in this policy.

## Security

We protect personal information using:
- Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest
- Database-level access controls (row-level security) ensuring each user can only access their own data
- No personal information in application logs or error messages
